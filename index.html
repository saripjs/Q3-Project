  • Accolades

Drake, whose full name is Aubrey Drake Graham, is a Canadian rapper, singer, songwriter, actor, and entrepreneur. He first gained fame as an actor on the teen drama television series "Degrassi: The Next Generation" before pursuing a career in music. Drake's music blends elements of hip-hop, R&B, and pop, with emotionally introspective lyrics often reflecting on relationships, fame, and personal growth.

He rose to prominence with his mixtapes, particularly "So Far Gone," which included hits like "Best I Ever Had." His debut studio album, "Thank Me Later" (2010), debuted at number one on the US Billboard 200. Since then, Drake has released several critically acclaimed albums, including "Take Care" (2011), "Nothing Was the Same" (2013), "Views" (2016), "Scorpion" (2018), and "Certified Lover Boy" (2021).
Drake holds numerous records in the music industry, including the most charted songs (over 230) by any solo artist on the Billboard Hot 100. He has won several Grammy Awards and has been recognized for his impact on music and culture. Aside from music, Drake has ventured into business with his own record label, OVO Sound, and has partnerships with brands like Nike, among others. He continues to be a dominant force in contemporary music, known for his catchy melodies, versatile style, and ability to consistently produce chart-topping hits.

    Record-Breaking Hits:

  • Drake holds the record for the most charted songs (over 230) by any solo artist on the Billboard Hot 100
  • He has the most top 10 hits on the Billboard Hot 100 chart.
  • His song "God's Plan" held the record for the most streams in a single day on Spotify.
  • "In My Feelings" became the first song to simultaneously top the Billboard Hot 100, Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs, and Hot Rap Songs charts.

    • Grammy Awards:

  • Drake has won multiple Grammy Awards, including Best Rap Album for "Take Care" and "Views."
  • He has received numerous nominations across various categories.
